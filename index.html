📖 How It Works

🏆 The Game

Predict the result of every World Cup 2026 match across 7 rounds — from the group stage all the way to the Final. The player with the most points each round wins.

🎯 Scoring
  • 1 pt — Correct result: Home Win / Draw / Away Win (regular matches).
  • 3 pts — ⭐ match: exact score on both teams (all rounds).
  • 2 pts — ⭐ Round 1 only: off by 1 goal, predicted UNDER (fewer total goals).
  • 1.5 pts — ⭐ Round 1 only: off by 1 goal, predicted OVER (more total goals).
  • 1 pt — ⭐ match: correct winner but score not exact (all rounds).
  • 0 pts — ⭐ match: wrong winner.
📢 Rule update (Rounds 2–7): At the request of the players, the ⭐ exact score match has been simplified starting from Round 2. Only the exact score awards 3 pts; getting the winner right (without exact score) awards 1 pt; wrong winner = 0 pts. The 2 pt and 1.5 pt tiers applied in Round 1 only.

Tiebreaker (same total points): the player who predicted UNDER wins. If still tied, the one closest on the losing team's goals wins — ensuring one winner per round.

⏰ Deadlines

Picks for each round lock automatically 5 hours before the first match of that round kicks off. A countdown timer shows how much time you have left.

📋 How to Play
  • Select a round tab (Round 1–7) at the top of the Picks screen.
  • Tap Home / Draw / Away for each match.
  • For the ⭐ match, also enter the exact score.
  • Hit Submit Picks before the deadline — picks cannot be changed after submission.
🔮 Knockout Rounds (4–7)

Until teams qualify, slots show their bracket position (e.g. '1st Group A'). The ? badge means the team is not confirmed yet. Names update automatically once teams are set.

📄 PDF Receipt

A PDF with all your picks is generated automatically when you submit. You can also download or print it at any time from the Picks screen.
